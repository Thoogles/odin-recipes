Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a great dish to cook as a dinner during the week, as it has enough food for multiple days after the dish is done! There will also be plenty of protein and carbonhydrates to keep you going throughout the work days.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef, 400 grams
  • Lasagna dinner kit
  • Cream cheese (grated), 200 grams
  • Bolognese sauce, 1 can
  • Extra lasagna sheets
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180C
  2. Brown the ground beef on a frying pan
  3. Add the lasagna kit sauce and bolognese to the ground beef
  4. Butter up the oven tray
  5. Cover bottom of the tray with thin layer of sauce, and then cover with lasagna sheets
  6. Cover the lasagna sheets with sauce, and add grated cheese on top. Continue this until tray is filled.
  7. Cook in oven for 20-25 minutes.
